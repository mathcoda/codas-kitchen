Green Mayo

A delicious mayo made with chives, dijon mustard and soybean oil (no eggs!).


    Ingredients:
  • 150ml Milk
  • 1tsp chopped Chives
  • 1/2tsp chopped Red Pepper (seedless)
  • 1/2tsp Dijon Mustard (seedless)
  • 1/2 Garlic Clove
  • 300ml Soybean Oil (or until it binds)
  • Salt to taste
    How to prepare:
  1. In a blender, put milk, chopped chives, chopped pepper, chopped garlic clove, dijon mustard.
  2. Mix it. Stop when it becomes a green liquid.
  3. Start the blender again in it's slowest speed and open the lid.
  4. Slowly add the oil in a continuous thread until it binds.
  5. Season with salt and keep in the fridge.
