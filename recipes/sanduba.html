American Sandwich

A classic sandwich with simple ingredients that makes for a incredible meal.


    Ingredients:
  • 1 French Bread
  • 1 Ham slice
  • 2 Cheese slices
  • 1 Egg
  • Lettuce
  • 1/2 Tomato
  • 1tsp Mayo
    How to Prepare:
  1. Put the ham and cheese in a hot pan until the cheese melts and place inside the bread.
  2. Fry the egg and place it above the ham and cheese.
  3. Chop some lettuce and tomato slices. Put it on top of the egg.
  4. Finish it spreading some mayo on the top bread.
