Boiled Egg with Runny Yolk

A delicious way to eat boiled eggs with just one simple trick.


    Ingredients:
  • Eggs
  • 6 Ice Cubes
    How to Prepare:
  1. Put some water on the stove until it boils.
  2. Gently, put the eggs in the water and wait 6 minutes and 30 seconds.
  3. In a bowl, put the ice cubes and fill it with water.
  4. After the timer is up, immediately put the eggs on the cold water.
  5. Wait 3 minutes to open the eggs.
